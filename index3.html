The 6-Module Blueprint

Twelve weeks of relentless building. Every week is a milestone, not a lecture.

Module 01

Problem Deconstruction

Kill your darling idea. Learn to separate "Cool Tech" from "Painful Market Gaps" through 50+ customer discovery calls.

Module 02

The MVP Philosophy

Master the "Value per Click" metric. Build and deploy a functional prototype using no-code or AI stacks within 14 days.

Module 03

GTM Micro-Experiments

Stop guessing where users are. Run low-cost growth experiments across LinkedIn, Meta, and cold-outreach channels.

Module 04

Unit Economics & Ops

Understanding the math behind the madness. CAC, LTV, and building a scalable operating system for your team.

Module 05

Narrative & Pitching

Moving from "What we do" to "Why we win." Crafting a story that creates FOMO among early-stage VCs.

Module 06

The Demo Sprint

The Final Stress Test. Pitching to a panel of operator-mentors and angel investors in a high-pressure simulation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to leave my job?

No. The program is designed for the "Pre-Commitment" stage. It is a high-intensity hybrid model (evenings/weekends) meant to help you validate your idea so you can quit with confidence by Week 12.

What is the "Founder Guild"?

The Guild is our lifelong alumni network. It provides direct access to warm intros with investors, monthly expert AMAs, and shared services (Legal, HR, Tech) at founder-friendly rates.

How do you select applicants?

Resumes matter less than "Proof of Work." We look for builders who have hacked together side projects, consultants with deep domain insights, or operators who have scaled products before.

Is there an equity stake?

We offer two tracks. The Execution Sprint is a fee-based track (zero equity). The Accelerator Track is for high-traction graduates where we invest capital for a small equity stake.
